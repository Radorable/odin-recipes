A bit of history

Dish of Swiss origin, but also popular in other countries, principaly in France. Melted cheese was originally consumed by peasants in the mountainous Alpine regions of the cantons of Valais and Fribourg (Switzerland), and Savoie and Haute-Savoie (France).

Ingredients

We only need a few ingredients to make this recipe, let's take a look!

  • As many raclette cheese as you want.
  • As many potatoes as you want.
  • sliced ​​bread (optional)
  • charcuterie (as ham, salami, chorizo, etc...).

Here we go, let's now make a mix!

Instructions

Now let's take a look about how making this.

  1. Get a raclette grill, then put your raclette cheese on it.
  2. Wait for the cheese to melt and then slide it with a knife onto a plate with a potato or a slice of bread.

And... That's all ! Very simple, but very delicious.
